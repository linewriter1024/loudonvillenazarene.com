Who We Are

We are a growing, caring, loving group of Christians who want to be like Jesus in our families, in our church, in our village and in our world.

Our Mission

We know God loves us and has called us to actively love others, so we will through all means possible represent Jesus so that others will be drawn to Him.

Ministries

Sunday School

Sunday School is offered every Sunday morning beginning at 9:30 a.m. We have an adult group, teen group, and kids group. Come join the fun as we learn more about God together and grow in community.

Connected Kids Group

Sunday evenings 6:00–7:00 PM. Connected Kids group is a safe space where kids are welcomed and shown the love of Christ through games, Bible lessons, and fellowship.

Our Beliefs

We Believe...

  • In one God, The Creator of all things, who reveals himself as Father, Son and Spirit.
  • In Jesus Christ, who is fully God and fully man at the same time, who became like us to bring about our salvation.
  • In the Holy Spirit, who is active in the world, bringing us to salvation.
  • That the Bible is the Word of God, giving us all we need to know about how to be saved.
  • That we are all sinners by both nature and actions and need God's forgiveness and cleansing.
  • That Jesus Christ died on the cross and that, but trusting in His death, we can be restored to a right relation to God and that He rose from the dead and ascended into heaven to prepare a place for us.
  • That God has enabled us to turn to Him from sin but that He has not forced us to do so.
  • That each person must repent, turn away from his sins, and trust Christ by faith to accept Him.
  • That when we turn from sin and trust in Christ, the old record of sin is wiped clean, and we are born anew, thus becoming part of the family of God.
  • That after being born anew, we need the fullness of God's Spirit in our hearts. When we make a complete commitment to Him, He cleanses our spirit, fills us with His Spirit and perfect love, and gives us the power to live victoriously.
  • In the Church, the community that confesses Jesus Christ as Lord, the covenant people of God made new in Christ, the Body of Christ called together by the Holy Spirit through the Word.
  • In baptism and urge people to be baptized as Christians.
  • In the Lord's Supper.
  • God can heal. We pray for healing. We also believe that God can work through medical science.
  • That Christ is coming again.
  • That everyone shall face the judgment of God with its rewards and punishments.

Staff Directory

Pastor Beth and Joe Heath, Peter, and Jacob

Lead Pastor

Pastor Beth grew up in Eastern North Carolina and is a graduate of Trevecca Nazarene University and Nazarene Theological Seminary. Her husband, Joe, was raised in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania, and graduated from Eastern Nazarene College. The two were married in June of 2013. Beth and Joe served for six years as missionaries with the Church of the Nazarene in South Africa before returning to the United States. In June of 2021, Pastor Beth began serving as the pastor of Loudonville Nazarene. They are the proud parents of two boys—Peter (2015) and Jacob (2022)—and share their home with their dog, Cocoa. As a family, they love spending time outdoors, playing games, and enjoying time with friends. As a family, they try to make the most of every day and choose joy no matter the circumstances.

Pastor Hannah and Ben Leskey

Children's Pastor

Pastor Hannah grew up a missionary kid in Southeast Asia and graduated from Mount Vernon Nazarene University. Ben grew up in Maine and moved to Ohio where he also graduated from Mount Vernon Nazarene University. They were married in May of 2024. Hannah loves discipling kids, preparing really fun games, and laughter. Ben and Hannah enjoy hiking, long roadtrips to visit family, and good dinners.
