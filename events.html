Vacation Bible School

Each summer our church holds VBS for children from pre-school through elementary school. The average attendance in the last few years has been approximately 50 children and volunteers. At VBS children will learn upbeat songs about God, scripture verses and Bible Stories that they can apply to their lives. A snack is provided. Recreation is also part of each session. For questions please call the church office at 419-994-3690.
